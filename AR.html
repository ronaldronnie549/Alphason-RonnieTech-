Sleep Paralysis: Causes, Symptoms, and How to Cope
Sleep paralysis is a temporary inability to move or speak while falling asleep or waking up. It occurs when the brain and body are out of sync during sleep transitions, leaving the mind awake while the muscles remain paralyzed (a normal part of REM sleep).
